# NeuralVid Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: January 20, 2026**

NeuralVid is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

### No Data Collection
NeuralVid processes all videos and audio **entirely on your device**. We do **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, videos, audio, transcripts, or other content.

- Video editing, transcription, and AI analysis happen locally using Apple's on-device frameworks.
- No accounts, logins, or cloud uploads are required or performed.

### Photo Library Access
NeuralVid requests access to your Photos library solely to:
- Allow you to select videos for editing.
- Save exported shorts back to your library (optional).

Your videos and photos remain private and are never accessed by us or third parties.

### In-App Purchases
Subscriptions and one-time purchases are handled securely by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

### Third Parties
We do not share data with third parties. Apple may collect anonymized analytics for App Store features.

### Children's Privacy
NeuralVid is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

### Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.

Thank you for using NeuralVid!
